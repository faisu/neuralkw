## Long description (500 words)

Enterprises processing high volumes of vendor bills face a compounding problem: AP teams spend hours manually matching invoices to contracts, rate schedules slip out of date, and vendor overbilling goes undetected until an audit surfaces it. Spreadsheet reconciliation and rules-only automation can't keep pace with the complexity of modern vendor portfolios.

neuralkw is an AI-native bill reconciliation platform built for this reality. The platform starts with agreement context — your contracts, rate schedules, SLAs, and renewal terms prefilled once and maintained as the source of truth. When bills arrive, whether from an AP inbox integration or bulk upload, an AI agent orchestrates reconciliation end to end.

The agent doesn't just apply static rules. It reasons over bill line items in the context of agreement terms, historical patterns, and confidence thresholds. Every decision is logged with a full audit trail. Exceptions — rate mismatches, quantity variances, billing period errors, duplicate charges, unauthorized line items — are classified, prioritized, and routed: approved, disputed, or flagged for human review.

Analytics close the loop. Finance leaders track exception rates over time, spend by vendor and category, agreement compliance scores, and reconciliation SLAs. The dashboard updates with every batch, surfacing spend drift that spreadsheets hide.

neuralkw targets enterprises with high bill density: utilities, telecom, facilities management, and organizations with sprawling SaaS vendor lists. The platform is designed for internal admin automation — reducing reconciliation headcount pressure while improving accuracy and recoverable spend.

Security and trust are foundational: audit trails on every agent decision, enterprise deployment options, and an active SOC 2 roadmap. Self-serve signup lets champions get started immediately and demonstrate value on their first reconciliation batch.
